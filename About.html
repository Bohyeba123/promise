    • Accounting

    Education in Banking and Finance aims at developing the necessary financial tools that will enable the
    individual occupy managerial positions in government and private sector establishments such as Commercial
    and Merchant Banks, Insurance Companies, Finance and Investment Companies, Brokerage firms, Commerce
    and Industry as well as succeed in self employment.
